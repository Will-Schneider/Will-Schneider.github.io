Fleet Bio:

We are a small fleet in the game Steel Ocean. We feel the need to rise against the oppressing fleets, such as; Dark Judge, DIIN, Bonkers, and Goons. These fleets have devoted themselves to reaking havoc in the game. We, the Confederacy, aim to break the grips of these fleets by providing a safe, friendly, and cheat free gaming environment. On our free time, we enjoy talking about WWII history. Specificaly WWII Naval conflict and tactics. If you feel the need to join the rebellion, just feel free to apply.
