Terms and Conditions.

These are the official rules and regulations of the fleet.
Any violation of these rules will result in an immediate expulsion from the fleet.
In some cases, you may be reported to the moderator of the game.

The Rules and Regulations

  1. Do not quarrel with members of the fleet.
  2. Respect other members and other fleets
  3. Do not purposefully team kill. This action will be reported to the moderators.
  4. Absolutely no use of cheat tools. Any use of cheat tools will be reported to the moderators
  5. Respect the chain of command within the fleet. Any insubordination will not be tolerated.
  6. If you are inactive for 5 months or more, you will be removed from the fleet. You may reapply, but if you become inactive again, you will be permanently banned.
  7. Try to gain rank fast and be a positive image for the fleet.
